Terms of use

LAST UPDATED · 2026-07-06 · PLAIN LANGUAGE, ON PURPOSE

The one-paragraph version: WILCO is beta software for a flight sim — use it fairly, don't abuse the relay or resell codes, and understand that a beta can break at the worst possible moment. When paid plans exist, you can cancel anytime and get a full refund within 14 days, no questions asked.

1 · The product

WILCO is an AI radio crew for DCS World that talks over SRS. It is an independent project — not affiliated with or endorsed by Eagle Dynamics, DCS World, or the DCS-SimpleRadio-Standalone project. It reads mission data through a standard export hook and touches no core game files.

2 · Beta means beta

The software is provided as-is. Controllers may mishear you, the relay may hiccup, and an update may change behavior between two flights. We fix things fast, but don't fly a competition finale on a promise.

3 · Invite codes & fair use

  • One code per pilot. Your code carries a fair daily allowance of AI calls that resets at midnight UTC.
  • Don't share, resell, or automate against your code. Don't attack, flood, or reverse the relay.
  • We can revoke codes that abuse the service — that's what keeps the beta free for everyone else.

4 · Accounts

Keep your password safe; we can't read it and can only reset it. Adding a recovery email is optional but strongly recommended. See the privacy page for exactly what we store.

5 · Paid plans & refunds

  • When subscriptions launch, billing is handled by our payment partner (a dedicated card-payments processor, named at checkout) — your card details never touch our servers.
  • Plans are priced by seats — how many pilots the crew serves in a session. Over-seat pilots get a polite notice and keep receiving all safety calls; only routine service waits for a bigger plan.
  • Cancel anytime; access runs to the end of the paid period.
  • 14-day refund, no questions asked — open an issue on GitHub or use in-app support with your billing email.
  • Beta pilots keep the price promised on the day they joined.

6 · Liability

To the maximum extent the law allows, our total liability is limited to what you paid us in the last 12 months (which, during the free beta, is zero). We are not liable for lost sorties, lost campaigns, or a wingman who heard "cleared hot" when the controller said "hold fire" — always fly the jet first.

7 · Changes

We may update these terms as the product grows; meaningful changes are announced in the changelog. Using WILCO after a change means you accept it.
