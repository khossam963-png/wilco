Under the hood

The parts you don't see — they're why it feels like a person and not a chatbot.

It hears like a wingman

Whisper STT is fed the live callsigns and station names each call, garbled station names get repaired ("over lowered" → Overlord), and mangled radio gets a human "say again" — never a guess.

Sub-second to ~1.5s replies

The local brain understands a call in under 100 ms, transcription starts while you're still pausing, and the voice starts speaking the moment your call is complete — with a squelch click when you're heard.

Never invents

Every position, frequency and threat comes from live data. Empty picture = "clean". Dead feed = "negative radar contact". No ghost traffic, ever.

Survives everything

Internet down → nothing changes, the brain is local. Optional cloud AI unreachable → the local brain takes over the same second. SRS restart → auto-reconnects. A controller never dies mid-mission.

One crew, one story

Controllers share a flight strip: Tower already knows what Ground told you. Handoffs come with the right name and the right frequency — from the comm plan, never invented.

Knows who's talking

Fuzzy callsign matching, self-renamed pilots tracked, two humans told apart by their stated altitude/heading, and pilot-to-pilot chatter ignored on busy freqs.

Any map, any side

Auto-learns every airfield on the loaded map with a generated comm plan. Flies blue or red. Active runway follows the wind.

One code, zero setup pain

Your invite code is the only credential — no accounts, no API keys, nothing to buy. Personality dial: pro, veteran, or full Ace Combat.
